Tokyo Twist

    Starters

  • Edamame

    Steamed soybeans tossed with sea salt or garlic sesame seasoning.

  • Gyoza

    Pan-fried Japanese dumplings with a savory filling of pork, chicken, or vegetable, served with a side of dipping sauce.

  • Shrimp Tempura

    Crispy battered and deep-fried shrimp served with tempura dipping sauce and grated daikon radish.

  • Tuna Poke

    Fresh diced tuna marinated in a sesame soy sauce, served with avocado, cucumber, and seaweed salad.

  • Takoyaki

    Traditional Japanese octopus balls, made with a crispy batter and filled with diced octopus, green onions, and pickled ginger, topped with takoyaki sauce and bonito flakes.

    Burgers

  • Tokyo Teriyaki Burger

    A juicy beef patty glazed with sweet teriyaki sauce, topped with grilled pineapple, caramelized onions, and melted Swiss cheese. Served with a side of wasabi mayo and crispy Asian-style fries.

  • Sumo Smash Burger

    SA towering burger with two beef patties, crispy tempura shrimp, spicy mayo, pickled daikon and carrot slaw, and a sprinkle of furikake. Served with a side of miso soup and edamame.

  • Samurai Salmon Burger

    A grilled salmon fillet with a sesame crust, topped with spicy mayo, pickled ginger, lettuce, and cucumber slices. Served with a side of seaweed salad and sesame cucumber salad.

  • Wasabi Wagyu Burger

    A luxurious burger made with tender wagyu beef, topped with wasabi mayo, pickled radish, arugula, and crispy fried onions. Served with a side of truffle fries.

  • Geisha Gochujang Burger

    A fusion burger with a spicy Korean twist, featuring a beef patty glazed with gochujang sauce, kimchi, sesame slaw, and melted cheddar cheese. Served with a side of kimchi fries.

    Deserts

  • Mochi Ice Cream

    A traditional Japanese dessert consisting of soft, chewy mochi (sweet rice dough) wrapped around a ball of ice cream in various flavors such as green tea, black sesame, strawberry, or mango.

  • Tempura Cheesecake

    A unique fusion dish where cheesecake slices are coated in tempura batter and deep-fried to crispy perfection. Served with a drizzle of raspberry or chocolate sauce.

  • Matcha Tiramisu

    A Japanese twist on the classic Italian dessert, made with layers of ladyfingers soaked in matcha green tea syrup, mascarpone cheese, and whipped cream, dusted with matcha powder.

  • Japanese-style Cheesecake

    A fluffy and light cheesecake made with Japanese-style cream cheese, which is less sweet and more tangy than its American counterpart. Served with fresh berries or fruit compote.

  • Kinako Pudding

    A traditional Japanese dessert made with soybean flour (kinako) and silky smooth custard pudding. Garnished with toasted kinako powder and whipped cream.

  • Green Tea Creme Brulee

    A classic French dessert infused with Japanese green tea, topped with a crispy caramelized sugar crust. Served with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or fresh fruit.

  • Red Bean Taiyaki

    A popular street food in Japan, taiyaki is a fish-shaped waffle filled with sweet red bean paste. Served warm and topped with a scoop of vanilla or green tea ice cream.

  • Beef Teriyaki

  • Miso Glazed Salmon

    Drinks

  • Tokyo Sunrise

    A tropical cocktail made with rum, coconut milk, pineapple juice, and grenadine, garnished with an orange slice and cherry.

  • Matcha Mojito

    A refreshing twist on the classic mojito, made with matcha green tea powder, fresh mint, lime juice, simple syrup, and soda water.

  • Wasabi Bloody Mary

    A spicy and savory cocktail with vodka, tomato juice, wasabi, Worcestershire sauce, and garnished with a celery stick and lemon wedge.

  • Sake Sangria

    A Japanese-inspired twist on traditional sangria, made with sake, plum wine, fresh fruit, and a splash of soda water.

  • Yuzu Margarita

    A tangy and citrusy margarita made with yuzu juice, tequila, triple sec, and agave syrup, served with a salted rim and yuzu twist.

  • Japanese Lager

    Classic Japanese lager with a crisp and clean taste, perfect for pairing with sushi or tempura.

  • Asahi Black

    A dark lager with a rich and smooth flavor, featuring notes of roasted malt and coffee.

  • Junmai

    Junmai - A classic sake with a full-bodied flavor and a smooth, clean finish, made from rice polished to at least 70% or higher, offering a good balance between sweetness and acidity.

  • Honjozo

    A versatile sake with a dry and crisp taste, perfect for pairing with a wide range of dishes, from sushi to grilled meats.

  • Yuzu IPA

    India Pale Ale infused with yuzu, a citrus fruit popular in Japanese cuisine, for a unique and refreshing flavor profile.

Specials

Teriyaki Glazed Steak

A juicy ribeye steak glazed with teriyaki sauce, served with a side of grilled vegetables and steamed rice. This dish offers a fusion of American and Japanese flavors, making it a perfect fit for an American-Japanese restaurant.

Sushi Burrito

A large sushi roll that's wrapped in a flour tortilla, filled with fresh sushi-grade fish, avocado, cucumber, and sushi rice. This dish is a creative take on the traditional sushi roll, making it a popular choice for those who want to try something new.

Chicken Katsu Curry

This dish features a breaded and fried chicken cutlet, served with a side of Japanese curry and rice. The curry is made with a blend of spices, including turmeric, cumin, and coriander, giving it a distinctive flavor that's perfect for an American-Japanese fusion restaurant.
